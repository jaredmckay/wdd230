Best Buy does a great job on contrast. Everything on the page is easy to read understand. They use color changes to help accomplish this, you can see this with the add to cart button and the pick up store botton. They have changed the color of text from white to black to compliment the yellow button. Any place you look on this website you will see great examples contrast.

White Space

Click here to see page

This page is great example of whitespace. You can look between the lines and appreciate this. Look how everything has appropriate spacing and gaps between the content. Having this intergrated into your design will allow for a webpage that is simple, that does not look clutered, and is easy to read and navigate.

Fitts Rule

Click here to see page

Aston Martin is a good example of implementing Fitts Rule. They have kept this page really simple. They show you a picture of a car and right underneath the picture is a button that takes straight to that cars main page. You look at the picture and you barely have to move your cursor to click on the link. This simple practice of keeping your button or link in relation to the content it is supposed to interact with will allow for a more intuative design, and better retention of visiters online.
